## üé® IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS

### **Technical Requirements:**
- **Resolution:** Minimum 3840x2160 (4K) for desktop
- **Format:** WebP with JPG fallback
- **Aspect Ratios:** 
  - Hero: 21:9 or 16:9
  - Sections: 16:9
  - Cards: 4:3 or 1:1
  - Mobile: 9:16
- **File Size:** Optimized < 500KB per image
- **Color Space:** sRGB
- **Compression:** Lossless for key visuals

### **Parallax Layers:**
Each section needs 3-5 layers:
1. **Background:** Slowest movement (0.5x scroll speed)
2. **Mid-ground:** Medium movement (0.7x scroll speed)
3. **Foreground:** Normal movement (1x scroll speed)
4. **Overlay:** Fast movement (1.3x scroll speed)
5. **UI Elements:** Fixed or subtle movement
