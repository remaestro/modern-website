## üéØ SPECIFICATIONS FOR ALL IMAGES

### **Lighting Requirements:**
- Infrastructure: Golden hour OR soft overcast for outdoor
- Tech/Office: Natural window light + minimal fill
- Control rooms: Even LED, cool-neutral (5500-6500K)
- Field work: Natural daylight, soft shadows

### **Color Palette:**
- Dominant: White, light gray, neutral backgrounds (60-70%)
- Infrastructure: Natural colors, green vegetation, blue sky
- Tech: Cool blues, greens (#52BA63 for status)
- Accent: Green #52BA63 strategically placed (10-15%)

### **Composition:**
- Clean backgrounds, minimal distractions
- 30-40% negative space for text overlay
- Organized, balanced, professional framing
- Sharp focus on main subject, graduated softness to background

### **Mood:**
- Calm confidence, not aggressive power
- Serene capability, not dramatic tension
- Professional precision, not industrial grit
- Modern excellence, not flashy tech

### **Technical:**
- 4K resolution minimum (3840x2160)
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- f/8-11 for infrastructure (depth)
- f/2.8-5.6 for people/tech (selective focus)
- ISO 100-800 max (clean quality)
- Professional cameras (Canon R5, Sony A7, Nikon Z9)
