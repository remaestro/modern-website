## ðŸ“ TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS (REFINED)

### **LIGHTING PHILOSOPHY**
```
PRIMARY APPROACH: Soft, Natural, Even

INDOOR SCENES:
Main Light: Large window or 4x6' softbox
  - Position: 45Â° from subject, slightly elevated
  - Color Temp: 5500K (daylight balanced)
  - Intensity: 70% (preserves detail)
  - Quality: Extremely soft (large source)

Fill Light: Bounce card or reflector (NOT another light)
  - Position: Opposite main light
  - Intensity: Natural bounce (30-40%)
  - Color: White/neutral (no color contamination)

Accent Light: ONLY if needed for separation
  - Position: Behind subject, subtle
  - Color: Very slight warm or green tint
  - Intensity: 15-20% (barely noticeable)
  - Purpose: Edge definition, not drama

NO HARSH SHADOWS: Everything should feel "open shadow" quality
NO DRAMATIC LIGHTING: Avoid film noir, high contrast looks
NO COLORED GELS: Except ultra-subtle green accent (5% intensity max)

OUTDOOR SCENES:
Preferred: Overcast day OR Golden hour (no harsh midday sun)
If sunny: Use large scrim/diffuser for soft light
Background: Clean sky or controlled environment
Time: Morning (8-10am) or Late afternoon (4-6pm)
```
